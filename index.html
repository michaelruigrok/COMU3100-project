Class is in Brisbane

In Australia, class is not very visible. We’re a have-a-go country where the concept of the blue-collar hard worker is idolised.

But don’t be fooled. The divisions of class are hidden, yet if you look hard enough you will find very tangible class structures. And these structures confine us in all sorts of ways, in all aspects of our lives.

So, what class do you reckon you fit into?

While class seems pretty straightforward, there's a lot going on under the surface that affect is directly. It might make you think very differently from your initial answer.

Your job is probably the first thing you think of when class is brought up.

And yes, the kind of job you have has a massive effect on your financial status. But it’s more than jobs that pay a little, and jobs that pay a lot.
